Individually Designed Instruction

For each tutoring session, the student will be provided with passages to read aloud. A comprehension test will follow each reading. Over time, the student will begin to steadily increase their reading fluency, as well as their comprehension. As this process develops, the student will be reading stories of increasing complexity. All materials are tailored to the interests, and needs of the individual student.
