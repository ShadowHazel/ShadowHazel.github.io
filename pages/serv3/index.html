Tutoring/Reading Supports

It is important that the student have a book of their choosing to read each evening. This will be part of the process of continued improvement. There is abundant research that indicates that students who read a minimum of just twenty minutes a night, increase their fluency and comprehension significantly, compared with students who do not.
